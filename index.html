About Us /

Here at Masanielli our mission is to handcraft the finest bespoke furniture from scratch; tailored specifically to meet your requirements all while using the help of our truly exceptionally talented artists.

Our team have been hand picked from different regions all throughout the world for their abilities and expertise in each of their chosen fields. The majority of which share a similar background and passion for sculptural art, which is then transfered over into furniture design.

Each piece is a masterfully crafted one of a kind creation that will exude a timeless and contempary feel in the home for many years to come...
